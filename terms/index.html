Crucial: Advance Payments Are Non-Refundable

To secure print materials, inks, and book custom schedules with our printers network, advance payments must be logged by admins. Once logged, all advance deposits are strictly non-refundable.

gavel 1. Price Change Tolerances

Estimated quotes provided initially in the catalog or upon order submission are approximations. During administrative assignment and setup, printing costs may fluctuate depending on materials availability.

20% Variance Rule: If the final negotiated cost differs from the original estimated price by 20% or more, a Price Change Notice will freeze the order and require explicit approval. If variance is below 20%, administrative logging proceeds without client re-confirmation.

fact_check 2. Data Confirmation Gate

All clients receive a summary text copy of their uploaded data upon order submission. Admins must log the customer's "CONFIRM" response before negotiation or printing workflow proceeds. Any corrections must be submitted prior to this confirmation.

preview 3. Proofing Rules

Digital proofs of the finalized prints must be approved via the Proof Approval page. No layout changes or corrections are permitted once printing begins, and JR Printiq holds no liability for typos or design errors present in the approved proof.

local_shipping 4. Timelines & Delivery

Turnaround durations listed (e.g. 5-7 days) are typical values representing standard completion schedules. Although we work to hit targets, turnaround timelines are never guaranteed to block unforeseen courier delays.
